What Is the Skeena Salmon Data Centre?

The SSDC is a centralized platform for accessing high-quality, open-access data related to wild Pacific salmonids in the Skeena River watershed.

Maintained by the Skeena Knowledge Trust, the SSDC connects researchers, governments, and communities with the knowledge they need to protect salmon and their habitats.
